Biography

Pierre Ramet is a full professor in computer science at Bordeaux University. His interests span high-performance computing, focusing on sparse linear algebra. He leads the team in charge of developing PaStiX, a high-performance sparse direct solver.

Research team: TOPAL (Tools and Optimization for high Performance Applications and Learning)
